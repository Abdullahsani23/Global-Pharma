WHO WE ARE

Global Pharma was founded in early 2005 in Indonesia. Through our vision and commitment to providing premium quality medical products with unsurpassed service, we have been able to grow without compromising our level of service. In May 2009, we set up our headquarters in Singapore and are now the sole agent for GP Care, COSMO Med and Winner Medical.

Global Pharma received the Value Creation Partner Award in 2015, Enterprise Partner Award in 2017, Group Buy Advocate and Customer Service Partner in 2018 from SingHealth. In 2016, the prestigious Singapore Enterprise Medal of Honour was awarded to Global Pharma in recognition of being trustworthy, reliable and outstanding.
